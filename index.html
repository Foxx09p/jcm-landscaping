Landscape Services

Clean up, refresh, or build out your outdoor space. JCM can help with planting, mulch, edging, trimming, bed cleanup, and other landscaping work. Once we know what you need, we match the job with an available contracted worker.

Landscaping work can be small, like fresh mulch around the house, or bigger, like cleaning up overgrown beds and getting the yard ready for the season.

  • Mulch, rock, and bed refreshes
  • Edging, trimming, and cleanup work
  • Planting and basic layout help
  • Seasonal yard cleanup

Pricing: Simple jobs may use package pricing. Bigger or custom jobs are priced with a quote after we see what needs to be done.

Holiday Lighting

Make your home or business look ready for the season without getting on a ladder. We can help with holiday light setup, takedown, and basic layout ideas when a contracted worker is available.

Holiday lighting can include simple roofline lighting, entryway lighting, or other outdoor areas that need a clean seasonal look.

  • Light installation and takedown
  • Roofline and outdoor display setup
  • Residential and small business options

Pricing: Pricing depends on the size of the home or building, the roofline, and the lights needed. Smaller jobs may use package pricing, and larger jobs get a quote.

Weed Control & Fertilization

Keep weeds under control and help your lawn grow thicker. We can set up weed control and fertilization services based on your yard, the season, and what your grass needs.

Good lawn care is easier when weeds are handled early and the grass gets the right treatment at the right time.

  • Weed control treatments
  • Fertilization for healthier growth
  • One-time and recurring service options
  • Plans based on yard size and season

Pricing: Pricing is usually based on yard size and how often service is needed. One-time visits and recurring plans can both be quoted.

Residential Lawn Care

Keep your yard looking neat without having to plan around it every week. JCM can help with mowing, trimming, edging, and basic lawn cleanup for homeowners.

Residential lawn care is built for homeowners who want the yard handled on a schedule that makes sense for them.

  • Mowing and trimming
  • Edging along driveways, sidewalks, and beds
  • Basic cleanup after service
  • Weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly options

Pricing: Regular lawn care can be set up as a weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly plan. One-time jobs and larger yards may need a quote.

Commercial Lawn Care

JCM helps businesses, HOAs, and commercial properties keep their grass and outdoor areas clean and presentable. We coordinate contracted workers for the schedule and scope your property needs.

Commercial properties often need steady service, clear scheduling, and a plan that fits the size of the property.

  • Routine mowing, trimming, and edging
  • Cleanup for entrances, walkways, and common areas
  • Service plans for businesses, HOAs, and rental properties
  • Seasonal options when available

Pricing: Commercial work is quoted because every property is different. Pricing depends on property size, service frequency, and the work requested.

Aeration & Overseeding

Help your lawn grow in thicker by loosening compacted soil and filling in thin spots. Aeration and overseeding are usually best in spring or fall, depending on your yard.

Aeration opens up the soil so air, water, and nutrients can reach the roots. Overseeding helps fill in thin areas so the lawn has a better chance to grow in evenly.

  • Core aeration for compacted soil
  • Overseeding for thin or bare spots
  • Spring and fall service options

Pricing: This service is usually priced by yard size. Small and standard yards may use package pricing, while larger yards may need a quote.

Leaf Removal

Leaf removal helps keep lawns, beds, walkways, and curb lines from looking messy during the busy fall season.

JCM can help with blowing, raking, bagging, mulching where it makes sense, and hauling away leaves for residential and commercial properties.

  • Lawn and landscape bed cleanup
  • Leaf blowing, raking, and bagging
  • Haul away service when available
  • One-time and seasonal cleanup options

Pricing: Leaf removal is usually quoted based on property size, leaf volume, and whether haul away service is needed.

Snow Removal

Snow can get in the way fast. JCM can help clear driveways, walkways, and parking areas when a contracted worker is available in your area.

Snow removal can be set up for one storm or planned ahead for the season, depending on what workers are available and what your property needs.

  • Driveway and walkway clearing
  • Small parking area clearing
  • One-time and seasonal options
  • Residential and commercial requests

Pricing: One-time snow removal and seasonal service options can be quoted. Pricing depends on the area being cleared and how often service is needed.
