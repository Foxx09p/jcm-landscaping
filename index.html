FAQs

Questions about the reviewed job network.

Are job requests public right away?

No. Requests stay private until JCM reviews and approves them.

Who can claim jobs?

Only signed-in contractors who have been approved by JCM should be allowed to claim live jobs.

Can customers still ask for help?

Yes. Support messages go into a queue so JCM can respond and track follow-ups.

Does the static demo save real submissions?

This downloaded HTML stores submissions in the browser. The deployed production version needs a database and server-side auth.
