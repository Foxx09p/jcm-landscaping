Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Introduction

JCM Landscaping Network helps property owners post outdoor work and helps approved independent contractors find jobs they may want to claim. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how it is used, and what choices you have.

We want the platform to feel useful without being invasive. Some information is necessary for the service to work, such as your email address, job details, photos, and address. Other information helps us review contractor applications, respond to support questions, protect the platform, and improve the experience over time.

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, Firebase Authentication provides basic Google account information to the platform. This may include your name, email address, profile photo, and unique Firebase user ID. We use this information to create your account, identify your posts, show your profile, and manage access to contractor or admin features.

When you post a job, we collect the information you submit in the job form. That may include your job title, service type, city, ZIP code, full property address, phone number, property size, preferred date, budget range, frequency, written description, and photos of the work area. Your full address and phone number are not shown to the open job board before a contractor claims your job.

If you apply to become a contractor, we collect application details such as your name, email address, phone number, city, ZIP code, service radius, skills, equipment, experience, availability, and your explanation of why you want to work with JCM. If you contact support, we collect the name, email, topic, priority, and message you submit. Firebase and related services may also collect basic technical and usage information needed for hosting, authentication, database operations, storage, security, and analytics.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to run the platform. That includes creating accounts, showing posted jobs, letting approved contractors claim work, sending job information to the right people, reviewing contractor applications, managing support tickets, and keeping the site safe.

We may also use information to communicate with you about your account, a job you posted, a contractor application, a support request, or a platform issue. We use job and usage information to improve the service, understand what features are working, detect abuse, enforce our terms, and prevent spam or unauthorized access.

What Contractors Can See

Before a job is claimed, approved contractors can see the public job details needed to decide whether the work fits their schedule. This includes the service type, job title, city, ZIP code, property size, budget range, frequency, preferred date, written details, and photos.

Before claiming, contractors do not see the full property address or customer phone number. After a contractor claims a job, the platform may show that contractor the full address, customer phone number, customer email, and other details needed to complete the work.

What We Share

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as needed to operate the platform, connect job posters with approved contractors, respond to support requests, comply with legal obligations, protect the site, or complete an action you requested.

JCM Landscaping Network uses Firebase, which is part of Google, for authentication, Firestore database storage, and Firebase Storage for uploaded job photos. Google provides the infrastructure that stores and processes this information for the platform. Google's privacy practices apply to that infrastructure, and users should review Google's policies if they want more detail about how Firebase services operate.

Data Retention

We keep account, job, contractor application, and support information for as long as it is needed to operate the platform, maintain records, respond to disputes, prevent abuse, comply with legal obligations, and improve the service. Some records may remain after a job is completed so JCM can understand platform activity and address later questions.

You can request deletion of your account information by emailing help@jcm-landscaping.com. We will review deletion requests in a reasonable timeframe. Some information may need to be retained for legitimate business, security, legal, tax, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution reasons.

Security

JCM Landscaping Network uses Firebase infrastructure, HTTPS, Firebase Authentication, Firestore security rules, and Firebase Storage controls to help protect information. These tools are designed to limit access based on authentication and role, such as customer, contractor, moderator, or admin.

No online service can promise perfect security. You should protect your Google account, use a secure device, and contact us if you believe your account or data has been accessed without permission.

Children's Privacy

JCM Landscaping Network is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or allow minors to create accounts, post jobs, or apply as contractors.

If you believe a minor has submitted information through the platform, contact us at help@jcm-landscaping.com so we can review and remove the information where appropriate.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information connected to your account by emailing us. We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request, especially if the request involves account access, job history, contractor status, or deletion.

You can also update some information by signing in with Google and using the site. If something cannot be changed directly through the interface, contact help@jcm-landscaping.com and describe what you want updated.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the platform changes, as Firebase features change, or as legal and operational requirements evolve. When we update the policy, we may change the date at the top of this page or provide notice through the site.

Your continued use of JCM Landscaping Network after a policy update means you accept the updated policy. If you do not agree with the policy, you should stop using the platform.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, data access, deletion, or how your information is handled, contact JCM at help@jcm-landscaping.com.
