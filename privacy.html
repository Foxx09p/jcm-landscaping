Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Overview

JCM Landscaping Network helps buyers submit outdoor service requests and helps approved contractors review nearby requests and submit quotes or interest. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who can see it, and what choices users have.

This policy applies to the website, mobile app, admin dashboard, GitHub-backed application data, Stripe payment setup features, and related support communications.

Account Information

JCM uses its server-side account API for account creation and sign-in. Users may register or sign in with email and password. Passwords are stored as salted hashes. Phone verification is unavailable until JCM configures an SMS provider. The account API may process your JCM user ID, email address, display name, and authentication status.

We use account information to identify users, keep users signed in, connect service requests and contractor applications to the correct account, manage roles, support admin controls, and protect the platform.

Service Request Information

When a buyer submits a service request, we collect the information provided in the service request form. This may include title, service type, city, ZIP code, full address, phone number, property size, preferred date, budget range, frequency, description, photos, latitude, longitude, and related notes.

Public request details may be shown to approved contractors so they can decide whether to submit a quote. Private buyer details include full address, phone number, email address, exact coordinates, and private access instructions. These details remain hidden from signed-out users, other buyers, unapproved contractors, and non-accepted contractors. After a buyer accepts one contractor and the post-acceptance workflow allows access, that accepted contractor may see the private details needed to perform the job.

Contractor Information

When a user applies to become a contractor, we collect application details such as name, email address, phone number, city, ZIP code, service radius, latitude, longitude, skills, equipment, experience, availability, and written application responses. Admins use this information to review, approve, reject, suspend, or manage contractor access.

Approved contractors may provide or allow access to location data so the platform can show nearby available requests. If latitude and longitude are not available, the platform may use city or ZIP code data for matching. If location data is missing, the Available Jobs page may ask the contractor to add or allow a service location.

GitHub-Backed Data Storage

JCM stores application data in a private GitHub repository as its current prototype data source for users, roles, contractor applications, service requests, quotes, messages, final offers, schedules, cancellations, disputes, reviews, contractor location, non-sensitive Stripe metadata, payment records, payout records, support tickets, audit logs, and uploaded photos.

Server-side API routes limit access based on account identity, ownership, role, contractor approval, accepted-contractor status, and lifecycle rules. Sensitive marketplace actions use trusted API routes. Git version history may retain prior versions of changed records and uploaded files. GitHub storage is not a transactional production database and JCM plans to move these records to a managed database before broader production use.

Stripe Payment Information

Stripe is currently configured for Sandbox / Test Mode only, so no real money is charged or paid. Contractor payment setup, payout capability, onboarding status, buyer test checkout, test transfers, test refunds, and payout history may use Stripe Connect. Stripe may collect sensitive payment, identity, tax, and bank information directly. JCM does not directly store raw card numbers, bank account numbers, or sensitive payment credentials in its private GitHub repository, local storage, frontend code, or admin dashboard.

JCM may store and display non-sensitive Stripe metadata such as Stripe mode, connected account ID, charges enabled, payouts enabled, onboarding complete, details submitted, requirements currently due, disabled reason, last sync time, payment intent ID, checkout session ID, charge ID, transfer ID, payment status, release status, refund status, and payout summaries when available from Stripe.

Admin Access

Admins and owners may view users, roles, contractor applications, service requests, quotes, disputes, support tickets, audit logs, and non-sensitive Stripe summaries. Authorized admins may reveal private buyer details for moderation, support, or dispute reasons, and those reveals are logged. Admins should not see raw card numbers, bank account numbers, or sensitive Stripe credentials.

How We Use Information

We use information to create accounts, authenticate users, receive service requests, show nearby available requests to approved contractors, support quote submission and buyer acceptance, reveal buyer details only after acceptance under the allowed workflow, provide job chat, manage final offers and schedules, review contractor applications, enforce suspensions, support Stripe Test Mode onboarding and payments, respond to support requests, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

We may also use operational data to troubleshoot, maintain security, improve workflows, investigate complaints, resolve disputes, enforce our Terms of Service, and protect buyers, contractors, admins, owners, and JCM.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share information when needed to operate the platform, process user actions, connect buyers with approved contractors, use GitHub and Stripe services, respond to legal obligations, enforce terms, protect safety, or complete a request you made.

Contractors may see limited public service request details before buyer acceptance. After acceptance and only when the workflow allows access, the accepted contractor may see buyer contact and address details needed for the job. Non-accepted contractors do not receive those private buyer details.

Cookies and Local Storage

The site may use cookies and browser storage to keep users signed in, remember preferences, display cookie consent, and support security. Accepting cookies also indicates agreement to the Terms of Service and this Privacy Policy, as stated in the cookie banner.

Data Retention

We keep account, role, service request, contractor application, quote, message, final offer, schedule, cancellation, dispute, review, payment status, support, and audit records for as long as needed to operate the platform, maintain records, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, comply with legal obligations, support payment records, and protect users and JCM.

You may request deletion or correction of your information by contacting JCM. We may need to retain some information for legal, security, tax, fraud prevention, payment, dispute, or legitimate business reasons.

Your Choices

You may choose not to provide optional location data, but contractors may need a service location to see nearby jobs. You may update account or contractor profile details where the platform supports it, or contact JCM for help. You may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information associated with your account, subject to verification and retention requirements.

Security

JCM uses salted password hashes, signed sessions, HTTPS, server-side repository access, role-based authorization, server-side Stripe endpoints, and admin controls to help protect platform data. No online service can guarantee perfect security. Users should use strong passwords, protect their phones and email accounts, and contact JCM if they believe an account or data has been accessed without permission.

Children

JCM Landscaping Network is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly allow minors to create accounts, submit service requests, apply as contractors, or use contractor features.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as the platform, storage configuration, Stripe integration, legal requirements, or operating practices change. We may update the date above or provide notice through the site when appropriate.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, data access, deletion, or how your information is handled, contact JCM at help@jcm-landscaping.com.
